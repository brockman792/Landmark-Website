Construction

From groundworks to keys in hand, Landmark Group manages every phase of new residential and commercial builds with precision and care. We deliver homes and structures built to endure for generations, on time and to the highest standard.

Full Project Management

We handle every stage from planning permission and architectural liaison through to structural completion and final fit-out, giving you a single point of contact throughout.

Groundworks and Foundations

Proper foundations are the beginning of every great build. Our site teams carry out all groundworks, drainage and substructure work to current Irish building regulations.

Structural Builds

Whether timber frame, block-built or steel-framed, our construction teams build to the precise specification agreed, with quality checks at every milestone.

Handover and Aftercare

We carry out a thorough snagging process before handover and provide full aftercare support, because the relationship does not end when the build is complete.
