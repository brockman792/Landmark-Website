Based in Dublin and operating across Ireland, Landmark Group Construction is a privately owned firm delivering exceptional builds across residential and commercial sectors. Built on a single conviction: every project, regardless of scale, deserves the highest standard of craft, care and execution.

Our reputation has been earned project by project. We understand that the key to success is forming strong relationships, providing full transparency and value-based solutions, and ensuring every promise made is a promise kept.
