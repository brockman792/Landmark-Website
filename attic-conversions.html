Attic
Conversions

Transform your unused attic into a beautifully finished living space. We handle structural engineering, insulation, electrics and finish to create a room that adds genuine value and comfort to your home.

Structural Engineering

All attic conversions require structural assessment and engineer certification. We manage the full process including planning, calculations and council submissions.

Insulation and Ventilation

Proper insulation and ventilation is critical to a comfortable attic room. We install to current building regulation standards, ensuring warmth, quiet and energy efficiency.

Staircase Design and Build

Bespoke attic staircases designed to fit your home, built with the same quality and attention to detail we bring to every element of our work.

Full Interior Finish

Plastering, electrical points, lighting, flooring and decoration all completed to a high standard, ready to use as a bedroom, study or living space.
